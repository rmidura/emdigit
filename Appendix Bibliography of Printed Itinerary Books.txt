Appendix: Bibliography of Printed Itinerary Books, 1545-1747

The following represents a bibliography of printed itinerary books from 1545-1747 identified as utilizing a route table format in part or full. Titles included in the “Itinerating Europe” article’s dataset are marked with an asterisk. There are many possible criteria for an early modern “edition;” for the purposes of this bibliography, differentiation in title, location, publisher or date as identifiable by union catalogues or individual consultation has been treated as a separate edition.
